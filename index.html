Greater Manchester Road Safety Audit (RSA) Procedure

These updated procedure have been developed for all Greater Manchester Partners to ensure that RSAs are carried out in a consistent and systematic way within Greater Manchester and sets out the key principles for undertaking RSAs.
They should apply to the Greater Manchester road network and for all other improvement schemes undertaken on Metrolink network and GM Bee Network Interchanges.
